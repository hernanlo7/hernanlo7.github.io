What to expect the work field to be like.

Professionals in information technology (IT) are tasked with assisting businesses in maintaining their digital infrastructure and assisting technology users with problem-solving. To assist others in keeping up with technology advancements and security protocols, IT professionals are in high demand.

Information technology experts research, create, implement, maintain, and support computer-based information systems, including both software and hardware.

Is I.T a good choice of a career field?

IT offers a variety of career prospects, competitive pay, work-life balance, and other benefits. Find out more about why a career in IT is an excellent choice if you're prepared to make a career transition. IT could be your best choice going forward due to growth, excellent salaries, and work-life balance.

According to the US Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual income for computer and information technology (IT) workers in the US was $97,430 in May 2021. That's a lot higher than the average wage for all occupations, which is $45,760.
